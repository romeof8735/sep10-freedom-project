Overview:

Technology can help not only speed up construction time but also help keep workers safer with these softwares and hardwares.

  • Construction has been around for thousands of thousands of years.
  • Depending on how much construction is needed it could take a few years to complete.
  • This website will inform you how technology can help speed up the construction process.

  • Consumer software LetsBuild: tracks project progression and gives reports Fieldwire: blueprint management software.
  • Consumer hardware Drone: Can visually inspect any area of land. Sonetics bluetooth headset: Allows for workers and managers to communicate in real time.
  • Advanced Drone: Can scan land and give a 3d descriptive perspective about it. It would be able to scan the land in under 1 minutes. The scan can even see underground to see if everything is stable.
  • The drone is also able to give project managers real time progress reports in one click of a button.
  • When the drone goes too far out of range it automatically fly back to the person in control.
  • Send real time pictures and videos straight to your phone.
  • Automatically delete duplicate pictures or videos.
  • Tech Safety glasses: These glasses are able to give workers information based on the project on a virtual heads up display
  • The virtual heads up display can also provide workers with persist calculations, measurements, etc.
  • These tech glasses would also be equipped with sensors that can detect if something is coming at the worker.
  • The glasses are also able to help the worker build where they need to and use specific tools via the heads up display to guide them.
  • Able to deem as sunglasses based on the sunlight present. Able to google something when you speak to it.
